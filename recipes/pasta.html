Description

Tons of penne pasta surrounded with tomato sauce and cheese and if you zesty then meatballs.

Ingredients

  • penne pasta
  • water(boiled)
  • zesty ahh tomato sauce
  • mozzarella or parmesan cheese
  • (meatballs if you like balls)

Steps

  1. boil the water on the stove
  2. place the pasta until al dente
  3. pour the water, but save some to put in sauce
  4. mix the pasta sauce generously top off with cheese (and meatballs cuz u zesty)
  5. plate that bitch and get a fork
